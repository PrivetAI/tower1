# Tower Climber

Tower Climber is an addictive idle clicker game where you build an endless tower by tapping the climb button. Earn coins with each floor you climb, unlock powerful upgrades to increase your climbing speed and automate your progress, and complete challenging milestones to earn bonus rewards. Watch your tower grow taller as you advance, with animated windows lighting up and visual effects celebrating your achievements. Perfect for quick gaming sessions or extended play, Tower Climber offers a satisfying progression system that keeps you coming back for more.
